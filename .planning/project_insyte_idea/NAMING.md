## Why insyte

- **Short** (6 letters), memorable, easy to say
- **Everyone knows** what "insight" means — no explanation needed
- **Works for both modes**: "Get insyte into hash tables" / "Get insyte into your code"
- **Sounds premium** — not cutesy, not generic
- **The "y" stylization** makes it unique and brandable
- **Great logo potential** — eye icon, magnifying glass, glowing node graph
